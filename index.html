ABOUT WEBDOLLAR

Webdollar is the currency of the internet. A Browser-based Peer-to-Peer cryptocurrency that aims to significantly increase the worldwide adoption of decentralized currencies.

Offering the possibility to send and receive payments online on any Browser, directly from one party to another, without going through a financial institution, while the confirmations of these transactions are secure on the blockchain without passing them through potential vulnerable channels like a Web Server or RPC intermediary as in BTC.

The wallets are stored directly in your browser. Your browser is the only one that is storing your wallet. NOTE: Like with any coin or token, if you lose your wallet, your WebDollars will be locked for ever. The wallets are also encrypted with AES-192 like in Bitcoin, requiring a 12-word password
The amount of WEBD you can mine in 24 hours is solely based on the computational power of your devices. WebDollar uses NiPoPoW, and there is a computation competition.
Each miner will receive a reward based on his contribution to global network hashes to resolve a block minus Pool leader fee.
