ABOUT WEBDOLLAR

Webdollar is the currency of the internet. A Browser-based Peer-to-Peer cryptocurrency that aims to significantly increase the worldwide adoption of decentralized currencies.







Offering the possibility to send and receive payments online on any Browser, directly from one party to another, without going through a financial institution, while the confirmations of these transactions are secure on the blockchain without passing them through potential vulnerable channels like a Web Server or RPC intermediary as in BTC.
